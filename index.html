Movie poster

About

Angus was released in 1995 and tells the story of an awkward, overweight, and self-concious teenager as he goes through a rough year in high school.

Importance

Although I did not know it at the time, Angus did not have a great reception on release. I am also sure that many people who view it today would also not give it much praise. But at the time I first saw Angus I was 8 years old and held many of the same "abnormal" traits that Angus, the character, had. I also saw these same traits in other people in my life.
